GI Pipes

GI pipes epitomise durability and reliability. These pipes are coated with a layer of zinc to prevent corrosion and rusting. The zinc coating acts as a shield for the underlying steel from exposure to moisture and other elements. They serve as indispensable assets, ensuring longevity and performance in every endeavour.

GP Pipes

GP pipes are made from high-quality steel and undergo a galvanisation process prior to forming, resulting in a protective zinc coating against corrosion. This makes them durable and ideal for various structural applications in construction, infrastructure, and agriculture. They offer longevity and cost-effectiveness due to their resistance to corrosion, conforming to industry standards for quality assurance.

CRCA Slit Coils

CRCA, short for Cold Rolled Closed Annealed, is a procedure typically employed post-hot steel rolling to enhance its hardness. CRCA Slit Coils, renowned for their outstanding quality and dependability, constitute a significant segment of steel coils. Employing a specialised cold rolling technique and annealing, these coils attain remarkable strength and surface perfection.

Cuplocks

Cuplocks are used both as high-load carriers and façade scaffolding. These are typically used in heavy laying and taller building constructions. We offer Cuplock Verticals and Cuplock Horizontals/Ledgers that are durable, easy to install and eco-friendly. Cuplock Horizontals/Ledgers are made from an NB 40mm ‘B’ Class Pipe and Cuplock Verticals are made from an NB 40mm MS ‘B’ class pipe made with a malleable cast iron top cup and bottom cup.
