About Us

Hariom Pipe Industries Limited

Established in 2007 by Mr. Roopesh Kumar Gupta, who serves as the Promoter and Managing Director, Hariom Pipe Industries Limited proudly honors the esteemed legacy of the late Shri.Hariom Gupta Ji, a pioneering figure in the steel industry since 1962. This illustrious heritage has been diligently preserved by the 2nd generation led by Shri Rakesh Gupta Ji & under his guidance the third-generation leaders Mr. Roopesh Kumar Gupta and Mr. Sailesh Gupta has excelled to become a leading manufacturer of iron and steel products in India.Representing the fourth generation, Mr. Ansh Golas has taken up the responsibility for the primary steel manufacturing facilities, continuing the legacy of trust and reliability.
